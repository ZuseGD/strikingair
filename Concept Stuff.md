### **Core**



* All 2d, similar to Darkest Dungeon in style



* Combat is an auto battler with different classes having rng biases towards targeting different positions with different effects. Character position also affects their targeting (eg frontline healer will not just spam heal)



* Party of 4 is randomly generated and set out on a (at first) randomly decided objective.(Find artifact, commit to crusade, kill boss)



* Resources will be retrieved from their investigation as the base grows from a hamlet to a full on large castle.



* Randomly generated features are randomly generated RPG stats like HP, Strength, Dexterity, Wisdom, Intelligence, Charisma. 



* Characters also have random traits that influence how they may act regardless of their stats. (Masochist, Dark Curiosity)



* Stat min-max parameters are randomly determined based on the character's class and race. Some classes may also have randomized abilities.



* Enemy parties are created with a similar idea in mind.



* The party will go through a variety of randomly generated rooms with combats/events/opportunities. The player will be tasked for figuring out which party members are fit to handle which events.



* If the party has groups of diverse races they may have to deal with racist people in the woods or other fantasy races seeking to look out for their own. Same can be said about classes like religious/non-religious characters.





### **Other Mechanics**



* List of Races:

  * Humans
  * Elves
  * Dark Elves
  * Orcs
  * Tieflings
  * Dwarves





### **Progression**



* Player will start off with a base selection of races, classes and garbage gear and from looting expeditions will find more. They will use xp gained from events to gain knowledge.



* As player progresses through events and "skill tree" they are able to recruit more classes, races, and buff up certain roles.
